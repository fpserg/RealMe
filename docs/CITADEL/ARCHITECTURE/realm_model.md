# Realm Model (Draft)

Status: Working document

---

## Current hypothesis

A Realm is not defined by size, importance or conversation frequency.

A Realm is an independent source of obligation competing for the Warden's attention.

Examples:

- Household
- Career

These Realms exist whether the Warden chooses them or not.

---

## Domains

Domains belong to a Realm.

They inherit attention rather than competing for it.

Examples:

Household Realm

- Stronghold
- Children
- Relationship
- Finance

Career Realm

- Research
- Leadership
- Strategy

---

## The Third Realm

Working hypothesis:

Unlike Household and Career, the Third Realm is entered voluntarily.

It contains activities that transform the Warden rather than merely demanding time.

Tower.

RealMe.

Reading.

Learning.

Sport.

Games.

Friends.

Creation.

Exploration.

Its true name has not yet emerged.

Current observation:

Household and Career are the more *Real* Realms.

The Third Realm is the more *Me* Realm.

Perhaps even more precisely:

It is the Realm in which today's Sergey gradually becomes tomorrow's Sergey.

This document intentionally remains unfinished until the right name reveals itself.
