# Protecting the Third Realm

## Working hypothesis

RealMe exists to organize the obligations of Household and Career so that time remains for the Third Realm.

Household and Career consume attention.

The Third Realm transforms the person.

Its final name has not yet revealed itself.

## Design Principles

- Organize obligations.
- Protect uninterrupted personal growth.
- Reward curiosity over productivity.
- Reward moments over checklists.
- AI should connect lived moments with culture rather than replace experience.

Examples

- noticing a hare
- discovering the Wooden Guardian
- drawing a sunset
- reading because curiosity appeared
- building Tower
- building RealMe
