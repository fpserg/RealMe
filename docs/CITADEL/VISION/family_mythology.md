# Family Mythology

## Purpose

RealMe should preserve not only events but also the names, stories and symbols that naturally emerge inside a family.

These become the emotional map of a place.

## Principles

- Do not invent mythology.
- Capture mythology when the family creates it.
- Every family has its own language.
- These names are first-class knowledge.
