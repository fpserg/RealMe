### Birthday Continuity

The Warden's birthday portraits follow continuous time rather than subjective life epochs.

Each birthday, RealMe may offer:

> "Happy Birthday, Warden.
> Would you like me to know what you look like today?"

The resulting portrait is added to the chronological portrait gallery.

Portraits are indexed by birthdays and age.

Epochs are never imposed in advance.

---

Birthday Experiences

RealMe should also support revisiting life through time.

Examples:

- "Would you like to revisit your 2026?"
- "Would you like to revisit your 40?" (4 May 2027)

Time is a first-class entry point into the Book of Life, alongside Campaigns, People, Realms, Places, Badges, Chronicles and Memories.
