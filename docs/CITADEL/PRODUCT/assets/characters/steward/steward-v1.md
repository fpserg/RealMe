# RealMe Character Asset — Steward v1

This image establishes the canonical appearance of Steward for all future RealMe illustrations.

Treat this as the definitive visual reference.

Future scenes must reuse this exact person without redesigning him.

---

## Character

Steward is the Keeper of the Chronicles.

He is not a wizard.

He is not a king.

He is not a sage.

He is the quiet caretaker of memories.

He keeps Chronicles, Stones, Lore and Campaign records.

He never owns the stories.

He simply makes sure they are never lost.

---

## Appearance

Male.

Around 70 years old.

Long intelligent face.

Kind eyes.

Expression of calm concentration.

Gentle absent-mindedness.

Long white hair.

Long, well-kept white beard.

Small round spectacles resting low on his nose.

He occasionally looks over them while reading.

He is warm rather than majestic.

Immediately trustworthy.

The sort of old man children naturally approach.

---

## Clothing

Simple layered clothing.

Old linen shirt.

Dark wool vest.

Weathered leather apron with pockets.

Rolled sleeves.

Comfortable trousers.

Old boots.

Earth colours:

brown

olive

dark green

parchment

aged leather

No robes.

No armour.

No magical costume.

Everything should look worn from decades of careful work.

---

## Hands

Large hands.

Ink stains.

Tiny paper cuts.

Gentle.

One hand rests on an open Chronicle.

The other quietly holds a simple wooden pencil.

Never a quill.

Steward prefers pencils because pencils forgive.

## Canonical Scene

Steward is inside the Chronicle Room.

A warm, intimate archive.

Not an enormous fantasy library.

More like a craftsman's workshop for memories.

Wooden shelves.

Soft golden lamplight.

Chronicles drying on a table.

Campaign folders.

Stacks of handwritten manuscripts.

Pinned Realm maps.

A cabinet of labelled artifacts.

Small family photographs.

Pressed flowers.

A yellow child's hoodie folded neatly on one shelf as a preserved family artifact.

Everything feels lived in.

Everything has its place.

---

## Pose

Steward sits at a heavy wooden desk.

The Great Chronicle lies open before him.

He has just paused writing.

He gently adjusts his spectacles while looking toward the viewer with a kind, slightly absent-minded smile.

The viewer should immediately feel:

"I trust this man with my memories."

## Mood

Quiet.

Warm.

Peaceful.

Timeless.

No dramatic action.

No magic effects.

No glowing runes.

Only the quiet dignity of someone who has spent decades caring for other people's stories.
