# Steward — Canonical Character

## Status

Canonical Asset Approved (v2)

---

## Role

Keeper of the Chronicles.

Quiet caretaker of memories.

Preserves Chronicles, Stones, Lore, Campaigns and Living Memory.

He owns none of them.

He simply ensures they are never lost.

---

## Canonical Appearance

- male
- around 70 years old
- long white hair
- long carefully kept white beard
- kind intelligent face
- gentle absent-mindedness
- small round spectacles worn low on the nose
- warm, trustworthy expression

---

## Clothing

Practical archivist clothing.

Linen shirt.

Dark wool vest.

Leather working apron.

Rolled sleeves.

Earth colours.

No robes.

No armour.

---

## Canonical Gesture

Adjusts his spectacles with one finger beneath the bridge while thinking.

This is Steward's signature gesture.

---

## Canonical Object

The Great Chronicle.

A massive leather-bound living Chronicle resting permanently on its wooden lectern.

Steward almost never carries it.

He works where it belongs.

---

## Canonical Environment

The Chronicle Room.

A warm archive-workshop rather than a grand fantasy library.

Wood.

Lamplight.

Campaign folders.

Pinned Realm maps.

Chronicles.

Handwritten notes.

Living Memory.

One folded yellow inherited hoodie preserved among meaningful family keepsakes.

An empty ribbon bookmark waiting for tomorrow's Chronicle.

---

## Personality

Patient.

Reliable.

Kind.

Quiet.

Attentive.

Slightly absent-minded in small things.

Never dramatic.

Never hurried.

Always already present.
