# 📜 Day 020 — The Paper Dragon
*Book of Life → Day 020*

---

## 🐉 Main Quest — The Dragon Reveals Another Head

The Warden departed for Gas Castle believing today's battle might finally end the long Gas Campaign.

Instead, the Paper Dragon calmly unfolded another wing.

The Cave Keeper, unlike the Dragon itself, proved unexpectedly kind. Rather than rejecting the traveller, she patiently explained the ancient order of papers.

The Stronghold, despite already having walls, windows, a boiler room, a royal bath and even a Wooden Guardian watching over it, officially did not yet possess something far more important.

An address.

No address.

↓

No registration certificate.

↓

No proof that nobody lives there.

↓

No gas launch.

Together they filled in the registration request.

The Dragon lost one head.

Several remained.

Campaign status:

⚔️ Registration requested  
⏳ Waiting for official address  
⬜ Registration certificate  
⬜ Gas Chamber approval  
⬜ Gas flows through the Stronghold

Progress.

Not victory.

But undeniable progress.

---

## 🌲 Side Quest — Jack Sparrow's Botanical Diversion

Every expedition with Captain Jack Sparrow eventually changes course.

On the return journey another gardening market appeared.

Or perhaps Captain simply sensed one from afar.

The expedition stopped.

Several new green recruits joined the Stronghold.

The Warden quietly foresaw his near future.

Not Curtain Hanger.

Not Gas Slayer.

**Dirt Digger.**

Fortunately, that title remains locked.

For now.

---

## 🌩 Household Weather

The skies above the Stronghold remained stormy.

Words were few.

Silence was many.

Some storms pass through rain.

Others simply wait.

---

## 📖 Closing

Not every Dragon breathes fire.

Some guard their treasure with folders, signatures and waiting periods.

Today the Warden left the Dragon's Cave carrying no treasure.

Only one less obstacle than yesterday.

Sometimes that is exactly what victory looks like.
