## Day 8 — The Storm and the Stone

The Realm shook.

Career remained calm. Denis's expanding task was completed under pressure, while the VIS Campaign remained paused by forces outside the Warden's control.

Household entered another storm. Fatigue, broken things and accumulated tension erupted into accusations. The Warden discovered an important distinction: responsibility and blame are not the same.

During the Evening Serpent, a new principle emerged.

> "The Citadel is never finished. There is only today's stone."

The Northern Estate moved one step closer to its identity. "Stronghold" became a serious candidate for its true name—not because of the buildings standing there, but because of what the place means to the family.

Before dawn another unexpected quest appeared.

The Gas Pipe Worker announced his arrival for tomorrow.

Another bottleneck prepared to move.

The Realm endured.

The Citadel gained another stone.
