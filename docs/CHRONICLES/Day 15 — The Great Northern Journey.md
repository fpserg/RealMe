# 🌧️ Day 15 — The Great Northern Journey

The rain arrived before the Morning Serpent had finished.

It immediately became obvious that today belonged to the Stronghold rather than the Tower. The destination had not changed, only the weather. Some journeys are defined not by where they lead, but by what they ask you to carry along the way.

---

## 🏡 Stronghold

**Weather:** 🌧️ Rainy, but advancing.

Today's campaign is the **Great Northern Journey**.

Roman returns to finish the plumbing, bringing another chapter of the Stronghold closer to completion. His bill waits to be settled—a reminder that every fortress is built one craftsman at a time.

An unexpected quest also appeared:

👔 **New Suit**

At first glance it looked like a Career purchase.

The Citadel judged otherwise.

The suit belongs to **Personal**.

Career merely benefits from it.

---

## 💼 Career

**Weather:** 🌤 Quiet.

Artem sent me his CV.

I suggested only one addition—a short paragraph describing his personal traits.

Once again I found myself walking the line between mentor and manager.

Helping him become stronger feels right.

Helping him leave does not.

One analyst's report also waits for review in the Tower.

---

## 🏰 Tower

**Weather:** Waiting.

The Builders Guild data remains untouched today.

That is acceptable.

Not every Realm needs to advance every day.

---

## 🌍 RealMe

Today's Morning Serpent produced an important architectural discovery.

The application may need only three permanent screens:

- 🏠 Home
- 📅 Today
- 🗺️ Map

Morning Serpent.

Evening Serpent.

Night Watch.

Fireside.

These are not screens.

They are conversation modes through which the Warden speaks with the Citadel.

That realization significantly simplified the product.

---

## 🔭 Observer

The weather changed.

The Journey did not.

Sometimes the only difference between an ordinary day and a memorable chapter is whether someone takes the time to write it down.
