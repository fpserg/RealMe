## Day 7 — The Hare and the Nest

The Warden moved through a compressed day of multiple Realms.

Estate measurements continued in coordination with Roman and Kadyr.
An unexpected night journey to the Northern Estate took place after Ivan fell asleep.

The journey was costly in time and fatigue but emotionally meaningful.
The Estate continues to evolve from construction site into lived home.

A wild hare was observed on the return journey.

A nest with nestlings was discovered under a pine tree, reinforcing the emerging identity of the Estate as a living place rather than a structure.

The concept of naming the Estate began to crystallize around themes of Nest, Owl, and Northern forests, but remains unresolved.

The Warden demonstrated a recurring pattern:
high cognitive load days can still produce net positive system advancement when broken into distributed Realm actions.

The Citadel continues to evolve as a parallel system of memory and structure.
