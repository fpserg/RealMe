# Day 11 — Open Roads

The day began with an unexpected Night Watch that turned into a long Saturday.

Household:
- Family expedition across Moscow completed three major Stronghold campaigns in a single day:
  - Dream Kitchen ordered;
  - Dream Bathroom ceramics chosen;
  - Dream Interior Doors selected.
- The purchases consumed most of the annual bonus, transforming financial savings into the future Home.
- Oksi's spirits rose noticeably, bringing rare sunny weather to the Household.
- Warden reflected that goodwill may sometimes be as real an asset as furniture itself.

Stronghold:
- Major procurement phase largely completed.
- Home became increasingly tangible rather than hypothetical.

Tower:
- Treasury automation architecture advanced.
- Data-flow scheme completed.
- Builders' Guild received request for `/check_coin ALL`; campaign now waits on an external dependency.
- Reflection led to a new principle: distinguish understanding from execution.

Foundry:
- GIFTED campaign revisited.
- Warden recognised a recurring pattern: designing sustainable systems, delegating operations and letting them live independently.
- Tower and GIFTED were recognised as campaigns within a broader realm of building ventures rather than isolated crypto projects.

Citadel:
- A new wing was conceived: the Fireside Library.
- Philosophy expanded:
  - RealMe is a language before it is software.
  - The Law of the Open Road emerged:
    walk while the road is open; leave blocked roads without guilt.
- July 31 declared as the Founding Feast target: a first working prototype of the Citadel, however small, should become walkable.

The day closed with the realization that RealMe should preserve not only the Warden's deeds, but also the conversations that taught the Warden how to see.
