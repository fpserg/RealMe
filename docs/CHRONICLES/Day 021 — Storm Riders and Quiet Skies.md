# 🌤️ Day 21 — Storm Riders and Quiet Skies

---

## 🏰 Household Realm

The day began quietly.

Owling gathered today's Stones beside the playground, wearing the same yellow hoodie once worn by Jr several years earlier. A small family artifact quietly continued its journey.

Soon afterwards a heavy summer shower forced a retreat from the Playground Quest. While hurrying back toward Stronghold, a pale rainbow briefly appeared exactly in the direction of home before fading away. The rain stopped only a few minutes before arrival.

Later the weather within Household also became warmer.

Oksi travelled to Moscow for her manicure together with the older kids, and before leaving we spoke and smiled again. Yesterday's field flowers seemed to have reached their destination.

Kadyr continued advancing the Bathroom Campaign.

Another Stronghold campaign quietly revealed itself after Sirius broke his leash once again.

He is not a chain dog.

Until a proper fence exists, Stronghold cannot yet become his true territory.

The Fence Campaign joins the "Later" backlog.

---

## 🌅 Stronghold

As evening approached, Stronghold stood quietly under warm sunset light.

The bathroom remained unfinished.

The garden had barely begun.

The fence still belonged to the future.

Yet for a few minutes none of those campaigns seemed important.

Stronghold no longer looked merely like a construction site.

It looked lived in.

---

## 🌾 Roadside Sign

On the way to the playground, two village lawnmowers passed by.

One wore a T-shirt with the words:

> "The best way to know the future is to invent it."

An unexpected sentence in an ordinary place.
