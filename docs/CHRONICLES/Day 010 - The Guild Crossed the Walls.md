## Day 10 — The Guild Crossed the Walls

Today marked the first campaign where the Builders' Guild left the Citadel to improve another Realm rather than merely record it.

The Tower Treasury routine was reimagined as the Tower Treasury Observatory (TTO): instead of manually updating spreadsheets, the Warden and the Guild began designing a system that gathers blockchain balances automatically and turns routine bookkeeping into meaningful oversight.

Career paused at a bottleneck after the VIS committee requested additional conditions rather than approving immediate participation. This unexpectedly opened space for Household and Stronghold campaigns.

The Gas Campaign advanced from construction to paperwork. The Household received welcome reinforcement through the annual bonus, although the weather remained unstable.

Warden brought sunflowers home. To him they were flowers; to Oksi they immediately became Stronghold flowers. The Realm crossed another invisible border: it no longer lived only inside the Citadel but began leaving traces in the household itself.

Champion scored six goals in training. During dinner the family helped a bee find its way back outside.

Late in the evening the Warden assembled a new vacuum cleaner and Ivan's chair by hand. Small victories, but true craftsmanship.

The Citadel no longer serves only as the keeper of Chronicles. Today it became a workshop for improving the world beyond its own walls.
