# PDR-008 — The Law of the Next Stone

## Principle

Every meaningful discussion must conclude by identifying the next physical stone.

## Rationale

Understanding and execution are different currencies.

RealMe exists to improve reality, not merely the map of reality.

## Rules

- Exactly one next physical stone.
- It must either:
  - be executable by the Warden, or
  - be explicitly blocked by an external dependency.
- Blocked campaigns are recorded and revisited later without guilt.

## Steward's Question

"What stone touches the world today?"
