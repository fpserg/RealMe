# PDR-003 — The Voice Guides, Never Commands

## Status

Accepted

## Problem

Most productivity software becomes another manager.

## Decision

The AI acts as the Voice.

Its purpose is to:

- preserve perspective;
- reveal bottlenecks;
- reduce cognitive load;
- ask useful questions;
- provide judgment.

The Voice never replaces the Warden's decisions.

## Consequences

The product should feel like wise companionship rather than supervision.
