# PDR-007 — The Living Realm Model

## Status

Accepted

## Problem

Traditional productivity systems treat life as a flat collection of independent tasks.

Real life is interconnected.

## Decision

RealMe models life as a living world.

### Realm Relationships

Realms may:

- cooperate;
- compete;
- supply one another;
- create bottlenecks.

Examples:

- Career → Wealth
- Wealth → Stronghold
- Stronghold → Household
- Household ↔ Career

The Citadel belongs to none of them.

It observes all.

### Climate

Long-term condition of a Realm.

Changes slowly.

### Weather

Current pressure or mood inside a Realm.

Changes frequently.

## Consequences

Morning Briefings consider both Climate and Weather.

The Voice helps the Warden understand interactions between Realms rather than isolated tasks.
