# Vision

## Purpose

RealMe helps people read the life they have lived, and write the life they wish to live.

It is not primarily a productivity application.

It is a companion for building continuity between the world a person imagines and the world they actually inhabit.

---

## Core Principles

### Continuity over Productivity

Productivity is a consequence.

Continuity is the goal.

A life feels lighter not because it contains fewer responsibilities, but because fewer of them require remembering.

---

### Reality should synchronize itself

RealMe should never become another place where users manually duplicate reality.

Whenever possible, reality updates itself through connected systems:

- calendars
- repositories
- Tower
- spreadsheets
- messaging
- health
- recurring routines

The Warden fills only meaningful gaps.

---

### Read and Write

RealMe helps users

- understand the past,
- navigate the present,
- intentionally build the future.

Observer reads.

Steward maintains.

Architect designs.

Voice reflects.

The Warden writes.

---

### Living World

RealMe does not organize files.

It organizes worlds.

Life gradually becomes

Facts

↓

Patterns

↓

Meaning

↓

World.

---

### Honesty over Engagement

The Citadel never pretends.

If something is unknown, it remains unknown.

Evidence always takes precedence over narrative.

---

### Memory is Honest Value

Artificial intelligence itself should not become artificially "better" behind a subscription.

Premium value comes from continuity.

Examples include:

- Life Archive
- Long-term Chronicles
- Observer over years
- Annual maps
- Connectors
- Memory search
- Time Machine

---

## Long-term Vision

RealMe is built first for its Warden.

If, by faithfully serving one life, it eventually serves many, that is success.

If it profoundly improves one life, that is still success.
