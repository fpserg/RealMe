# 10 — Roadmap

# Purpose

Define the architectural evolution of RealMe beyond the MVP.

The Roadmap describes capability growth rather than implementation schedules.

It is intentionally independent of release dates and development velocity.

---

# Guiding Principle

The MVP validates cognition.

Everything beyond the MVP extends capability without changing the architectural foundation.

---

# MVP

## Objective

Validate that a continuously maintained World Model reduces cognitive load while preserving human agency.

Core capabilities:

- conversational interaction;
- Morning Serpent;
- Living Inputs;
- continuous World Model reconciliation;
- World Model persistence;
- commitment tracking;
- WBT;
- Chronicles.

Success is measured by usefulness rather than feature count.

---

# Phase 2 — Personal Operating System

Objective

Expand RealMe from persistent memory into an operational assistant.

Potential capabilities:

- richer commitment management;
- recurring routines;
- reminder generation;
- calendar integration;
- email integration;
- improved temporal reasoning;
- cross-project reasoning.

The World Model remains the central source of truth.

---

# Phase 3 — Connected Life

Objective

Allow RealMe to observe more of the Warden's operating environment.

Potential capabilities:

- document ingestion;
- travel diary import;
- historical Chronicle import;
- file understanding;
- messaging integrations;
- passive signal collection.

Information should continue to improve the World Model through reconciliation rather than direct storage.

---

# Phase 4 — Reflective Intelligence

Objective

Develop deeper long-term understanding.

Potential capabilities:

- behavioral pattern discovery;
- relationship evolution;
- long-term preference extraction;
- strategic trend detection;
- cognitive drift detection.

The system should increasingly explain why it believes something rather than merely storing it.

---

# Phase 5 — Life Companion

Objective

Provide continuous support across all Realms.

Potential capabilities:

- proactive reasoning;
- scenario analysis;
- long-term planning;
- opportunity detection;
- integrated life review.

The Warden remains the decision maker.

Agency is never delegated.

---

# Recognition

Meaningful contribution recognition may evolve gradually.

Possible future additions include:

- Chronicle decoration;
- commemorative milestones;
- cosmetic progression;
- contribution history.

Recognition should always celebrate improved understanding rather than application usage.

Functional advantages should never depend on progression.

---

# Possible Future Research

The following areas are intentionally outside the current roadmap but remain architecturally compatible:

- knowledge graphs;
- local AI inference;
- multimodal understanding;
- wearable integrations;
- autonomous background observation;
- collaborative World Models.

These should be evaluated only after the core architecture has proven itself.

---

# Roadmap Philosophy

Every new capability should satisfy at least one of the following:

- reduce cognitive load;
- improve continuity;
- deepen the World Model;
- strengthen reasoning;
- preserve human agency.

Features that satisfy none of these should not be added.

---

# Completion Criterion

The roadmap has no final stage.

RealMe is expected to evolve continuously while preserving the architectural principles defined in this repository.

Every future version should remain recognizable as the same system, even if every implementation detail changes.
