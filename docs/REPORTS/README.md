# Reports

Reports are compressed views of the Chronicles.

Their purpose is not to prove productivity.

Their purpose is to preserve an objective record of how the Realm evolved.

## Levels

### Weekly

Operational summary.

Answers:

> What moved this week?

Audience:

- Warden
- Managers
- Family
- The Voice

### Monthly

Realm-level progress.

Answers:

> What changed this month?

### Yearly

The Annals.

Answers:

> What kind of Warden did this year forge?

## Principle

Chronicles tell stories.

Reports compress them into facts.

The Voice should eventually generate Reports automatically from the Chronicles.
