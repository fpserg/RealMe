# Tower Treasury Observatory (TTO)

## Vision

Transform Treasury management from manual bookkeeping into continuous observation.

The Observatory should automatically collect data, compare assets with liabilities and highlight only meaningful changes.

The Warden should never need to manually copy balances between systems.

---

## Responsibilities

- Collect blockchain balances.
- Collect in-game liabilities.
- Compare both.
- Calculate reserve coverage.
- Detect unusual growth.
- Highlight anomalies.
- Produce concise insights.

---

## Future Examples

🟢 All reserves healthy.

🟡 DOGS liabilities +18% this week.

🟠 NOT reserve coverage approaching threshold.

🔴 Exchange pair DOGS→NOT may create withdrawal pressure.
