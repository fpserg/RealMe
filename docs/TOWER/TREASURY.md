# Tower Treasury

## Purpose

Maintain confidence that in-game liabilities remain safely backed by real blockchain assets.

The Treasury exists to answer one question:

"Can every player who wishes to withdraw safely do so?"

---

## Components

Assets

- Real tokens on TON blockchain
- Multiple wallets

Liabilities

- Tokens held on player accounts
- Staking balances
- Dividend balances
