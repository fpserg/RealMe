## Owling

Owling's current favourite cartoon is **Booba**.

His first clearly distinguishable word was not "mummy" or "daddy."

It was:

> **"Booba."**

The resemblance is difficult to ignore.

Like Booba, Owling approaches the world through curiosity rather than caution.

Every room, playground or unfamiliar object is an invitation to investigate.

Things occasionally come apart.

Sometimes they can be put back together.

Sometimes they become today's greatest mystery.

For the Warden, Booba has quietly become another way of understanding Owling.

### Great Boiler Hall

Owling's favourite room in the Stronghold is the **Great Boiler Hall**.

Officially, it is a technical room full of pipes, valves, gauges, tanks and mysterious machinery.

To Owling, it is a place of endless discoveries.

Every handle wants to be turned.
Every pipe deserves investigation.
Every button is an unanswered question.

The Warden suspects that part of its fascination comes from a simple rule:

> "You are not allowed in there."

Like many explorers before him, Owling is irresistibly drawn toward the doors marked *Do Not Enter*.
