# Stronghold Lore

## Purpose

Collect places, creatures, names and symbols that naturally become part of the Stronghold's identity.

Unlike the Chronicles, this document is timeless.

---

## Named Places

- 🏰 The Stronghold
- 🪺 The Nest
- 🛁 The Royal Bath Palace
- 🗿 Wooden Guardian
- 🧙 Baba Yaga's Hut

---

## Living Inhabitants

### 🐇 The Hare

Frequently appears near the Stronghold.
Represents quiet welcome and peaceful mornings.

### 👑 Shakti

Bengal princess.

Immediately claimed the Royal Bath Palace before humans could install it.

### 🐦 Jack Sparrow

Symbolic name for the visiting sparrows around the Nest.

One sparrow striking the Stronghold window and surviving may eventually become part of this lore if the motif continues to recur.

---

## Principle

Nothing is invented.

Lore is only added after life repeats it or gives it meaning.
